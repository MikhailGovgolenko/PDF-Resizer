<h1 align="center">PDF Resizer</h1>

<p align="center">Web version: https://mikhailgovgolenko.github.io/PDF-Resizer/</p>

A utility to analyze page aspect ratios and resize PDF documents without quality loss.

Available as a web application and a portable Windows desktop application.

<img width="1536" height="863" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2e23ca02-1665-436b-83b7-137fe8819c05" />

### How to use:

1. **Open** PDF Resizer.
2. **Upload** your document by dragging and dropping it into the upload area, or by clicking the area to select a file.
3. Click **Analyze** to view the current page proportions in the log window.
4. Select your target proportions using predefined presets or a custom aspect ratio parameter.
5. Click **Download PDF** and choose a destination for your resized file.

---

The Windows version is fully standalone, requires no installation, and leaves no traces on your system.
